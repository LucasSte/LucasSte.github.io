About me

Hey 👋 I’m Lucas! I work on the Solang compiler to make Solidity available as a programming language for Solana. I’m very interested in software infrastructure, especially compilers applied to parallel programming and artificial intelligence frameworks.

I graduated from the Aeronautics Institute of Technology in computer engineering, and I currently work for Solana Labs as a compiler engineer.

During my free time, I like reading non-fiction, practicing sports (surfing and skateboarding) and coding.
